How to prevent fire accidents

Never leave your kitchen unnatended whilst cooking

Leaving your kitchen unnatended is just a welcome to a lot of accidents. You need to check your kitchen while cooking because it can cause a fire accident while you are not looking.

Inspect your heating sources

You need to make sure that your home heating sources are working properly or else this can start a fire accident.

Be cautious with open flames

You do not need to avoid campfires or candles but you need to be careful with these. Make sure to be cautious or else you might start a fire.

Maintain your wires

Make sure that before you plug into a socket, it isn't chewed or torn up. Damaged wires are very dangerous and can start fire accidents. Make sure to replaced damaged wires ASAP.

Prepare a fire extinguisher or blanket around

Worst comes to worst you get a fire accident you will need a fire extinguisher or a blanket to stop it before it gets too late. Keeping this around will help stop the fire before it spreads.
